About Me

  • Greetings! I am Abdulla All Noman, a dedicated professional with a strong academic background in Computer Science and Engineering. I successfully completed my undergraduate studies at North South University, Dhaka, Bangladesh, and I am currently pursuing my Master's in Data Science at Montclair State University in New Jersey, USA.

  • During my time as a Research Assistant at North South University from 2021 to 2023, under the guidance of Dr. M. Rashedur Rahman in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, I actively contributed to innovative research initiatives. Additionally, my role as a Teaching Assistant in the Department of Mathematics and Physics from 2019 to 2021 allowed me to hone my skills in effective communication and knowledge dissemination.

  • My current focus revolves around text mining and text analysis utilizing Natural Language Processing in the realm of Data Science. In a notable project, I spearheaded the development of a collaborative platform for medical institutions. This initiative aimed at classifying chest diseases using federated learning, with the integration of blockchain technology to enhance trust among stakeholders.

  • My diverse skill set includes expertise in machine learning, deep learning, and data mining. I have consistently sought opportunities to integrate blockchain technology across various disciplines, showcasing my versatility and commitment to exploring cutting-edge solutions.

  • I am enthusiastic about contributing to the ever-evolving field of technology and am eager to leverage my skills and experiences in future endeavors.
